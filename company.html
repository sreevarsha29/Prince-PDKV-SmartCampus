General Guidelines

Eligibility Criteria

Standard requirements applicable across most recruiting companies

Academic

  • Min 60% in Class X & XII
  • CGPA ≥ 6.0 (varies per company)
  • No active arrears/backlogs
  • All semester results submitted

Attendance & Conduct

  • Minimum 75% attendance
  • No disciplinary actions
  • Registered with Placement Cell
  • Attended mandatory orientation

Technical Skills

  • DSA & problem solving basics
  • Core CS / Engineering subjects
  • Aptitude & reasoning ability
  • Good communication skills

Documents Required

  • Updated résumé (1 page preferred)
  • All semester mark sheets
  • Aadhar / ID proof
  • Recent passport size photo
