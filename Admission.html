Eligibility Criteria

🎓 UG Programmes
  • Passed 10+2 / HSC or equivalent
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Age: 17–22 years as on July 1
  • Valid entrance exam score (if applicable)
B.Sc · B.Com · B.A · BCA · B.Tech
📜 PG Programmes
  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field
  • Minimum 55% aggregate marks
  • Valid GATE/NET/University Entrance (for select courses)
  • Work experience preferred (MBA)
M.Sc · M.Com · MBA · MCA · M.Tech
🔬 Research / PhD
  • PG degree with 60% marks
  • Valid UGC-NET / CSIR-NET / GATE
  • Research proposal required
  • Interview by faculty committee
Ph.D · M.Phil · Research Fellow
