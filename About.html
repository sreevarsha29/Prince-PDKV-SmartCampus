Our Story

Welcome to PDKV

Established in 2009 by Prince Educational Society, Prince Dr. K. Vasudevan College of Engineering and Technology is committed to delivering quality technical education in a serene 65-acre campus in Ponmar, Chennai.

Affiliated to Anna University and approved by AICTE, we offer UG and PG programmes across six engineering disciplines, blending academic rigour with real-world readiness.
